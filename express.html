Workout Tracker

Initial Login Screen

The Workout Tracker is an app designed to make organizing workouts easier. Have you ever wasted time at the gym looking for the correct weight or forgotten how many sets and reps you wanted to do? This app aims to solve these issues by allowing users to add each exercise individually and view them by the day they plan to do it.

In addition, users can easily update each exercise when they increase the weight or distance they are lifting or running. I built this app using JavaScript, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Mongoose, Bootstrap, and CSS.

The Workout Tracker is a simple yet effective tool for anyone looking to improve their fitness routine. With its user-friendly design, it allows users to keep track of their progress and plan their workouts with ease.

In the future I see many possibilities for growth, including adding dates of creation, implementing comments, and providing the ability to delete moves from specific days.
