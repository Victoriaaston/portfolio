Astren

Shop All Page

Astren is an e-commerce shop that brings creativity and passion together, created by three lifelong friends who turned their hobby into a business. As my final project for General Assembly, I built the Astren app to support my friends and provide them with a platform to sell their art.

The app is still a work in progress, but I invite you to check out the art and the application that I have built so far. For the front end, I used React, JavaScript, and CSS, while for the back end, I used Express, Node.js, MongoDB, and Mongoose. To facilitate payments, I integrated Stripe API into the app.

My current focus is on getting the app deployed, and I expect it to be available no later than March 8th.
